Breed History

The briard, which is believed to have originated in France, has been bred for centuries to
herd and to protectsheep. The breed was used by the French Army as sentries,
messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because ofits fine sense of hearing
Briards were used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction. Currently the
population of brands is slowly recovering. Charlemagne, Napoleon, Thomas Jefferson
and Tatayette all ownedbriards.
